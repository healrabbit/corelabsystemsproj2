So Hot You're Hurting My Feelings by Caroline Polacheck

Released: September 16, 2019

Genre: Synth Pop

This song reminds me of my old best friend. Shoutout them. We used to bump it in the car together and laugh about the lyric "Show me the banana" . A little while after moving to New York for school we stopped talking completely. I think they dislike me. I'm not sure why. The song is still good.
