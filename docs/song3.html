А​г​a​т​к​а (Agatha! You're Being Melodramatic) by Weatherday

Released: April 29, 2019

Genre: Indie Rock/Noise Pop

Oh where do I even start. Come In by Weatherday is first and foremost a painting album. I turn it on on loop to zone out while I paint. But this song is more than that. This is a song that brings hope in desperate times. A reminder that a poorly drawn person can still be loved. In a similar vein to pure, this song is like a soft friend.
