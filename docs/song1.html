beast friend by Jane Remover

Released: November 13, 2024

Genre: Electronic/Digicore

This song was off the project Teen Week. Teen Week changed my life. I was in the listening party when it dropped, and it inspired so many people in the little underground community the genre it was a part of was forming. I wrote my college admission essay about Teen Week. This song is special because it's such a picture of my teen experience. Feeling insecure and underappreciated in my real life small town while trying to make a name for myself creatively online. All official releases of this song were sadly removed from the internet when the album became abridged.
